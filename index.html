About

The name is Anna Sylvester. Those close to me call me “Anners” or “Ners.” If you are reading this, we can officially be close. Perusing this site will give you a better idea of who I am and what I can do. I graduated from Furman University in May of 2018 with a BA in graphic design. I could talk about Gestalt Theory or fonts for hours and to some of my friends’ dismay, I have. After graduating, I entered into the world of digital marketing at an Atlanta start up. My favorite projects were working side-by-side with the company’s web developer. With the team encouraging me, I started to teach myself to code in my free time. Sometimes it just takes one person telling you, “you can do it.” In November of 2018, I went ahead and jumped head first into the world of coding by attending the Full-Stack Development Bootcamp at GA Tech. I graduate in May of 2019. The more interesting things about me include: I have a pet hedgehog named Guy. I am an expert at whistling. I am a ~sour~ craft beer connoisseur. And I tie dye in my spare time.
